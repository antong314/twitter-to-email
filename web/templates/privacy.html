Privacy Policy

We believe in full transparency. Here is exactly what we do with your data.

What we collect

We only collect two things:

  • Your Email Address: So we can send you the daily digest.
  • Your X (Twitter) Handle: So we know which accounts to check for updates.

How we use it

We use your handle to look up who you follow on X. Once a day, we check their recent tweets and compile them into an email for you. That's it.

No Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with anyone. We use a transactional email provider (Resend) solely to deliver your daily email.

No Tracking

We do not track your clicks, your opens, or your reading habits. The emails are clean HTML with no spy pixels.

Your Control

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the link at the bottom of any email. When you unsubscribe, we immediately delete your email and handle from our active database.
